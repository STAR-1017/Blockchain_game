BACKGROUND


August 15, 1971 was the starting point for everything. On this day, the federal government of the United States stopped the conversion of US dollar bills and gold at a fixed ratio of 1 oz : $35, which eliminated the value of paper bills and accelerated financial capitalism.

Being dominated by people's greed, the gap between those who succeed in financial markets and those who cannot benefit from them has been wider over the years.

Bubble burst, Lehman shock, Euro crisis, Covid-19 pandemic

With each repeated financial crisis, even larger bills were scattered and continued to postpone the problem, resulting in continued capital polarization. Against this backdrop, engineers from around the world who share the ideas of Satoshi Nakamoto have developed decentralized Bitcoin and blockchain.

Blockchain is the key to solving various problems in the present world. Decentralization stops the polarization of capital and creates a truly equitable society.

What is CRYPTO APE COSIETY (CAS)?

It is an academic society that is familiar with blockchain and aims to spread it to all humankind over the world. It is a special academic society in which only 9999 specially selected people can be participated. From the current financial capitalism, which is becoming more polarized, to a centralized society where only vested interests are enriched, to a decentralized society, true fairness. Aim for a good society.

At the heart of decentralization is the cryptocurrency, the crypto market, and it is the role of CAS to achieve maximum success in this market and spread blockchain around the world.

Members can receive special information on their success in the crypto market every day from a dedicated page using their own keys, allowing them to make more profitable investments in the crypto market. Furthermore, by sharing information among members and synergizing with members who achieve higher results (MAM), we will gain great fruit in the market.

To become a member, you need to purchase a limited number of 9999 NFTs for $180, which will be used to run the academy, and also support the countries that have been severely damaged by the pandemic. It will be used to improve their food and blockchain knowledge and practice for the support activities of the people. Words of gratitude from those who received support from the activity, and new NFTs created there will be distributed to members on an irregular basis.

If you want to withdraw from an academic society, you can sell your rights on various NFT platforms, but you will lose the rights you get at the academic society.
